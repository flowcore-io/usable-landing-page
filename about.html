Knowledge Management for AI Agents

Usable Sp/f provides plug-and-play knowledge management that makes AI agents actually useful. We're building the shared memory layer that enables agents to access structured, persistent knowledge across systems and sessions.

Our Story

When we founded Flowcore, we were solving a specific technical problem: how to build event-driven data infrastructure that could handle enterprise-scale requirements. We succeeded. Our platform powers real-world systems managing critical data flows for customers across sectors.

But over the past year, something profound shifted in how we think about the problem we're solving. We started noticing a pattern as organizations began deploying AI agents: AI agents are terrible at knowledge management.

Teams building AI agents face a fundamental choice: build their own RAG infrastructure—requiring decisions on vector databases, embedding models, chunking strategies, re-rankers, and multi-agent orchestration—or settle for agents with no long-term memory at all.

This isn't a technical limitation of the AI models themselves. It's a knowledge infrastructure problem. Every agent conversation starts from zero because there's no shared memory layer.

We realized that the most valuable thing we could build wasn't just a data infrastructure platform—it was a dedicated knowledge base for AI agents. We built Usable on top of our Flowcore infrastructure to solve exactly this: providing AI agents with structured, long-term memory that persists across conversations and sessions.

What We Do

Usable Sp/f comprises three complementary offerings, each serving different needs in the AI and data ecosystem:

Primary Focus

Usable

Knowledge management for AI agents. A plug-and-play service for those building their own AI agents who need a knowledge management solution that just works.

  • MCP integration for seamless agent connectivity
  • REST API for custom integrations
  • Workspace and fragment model for organized knowledge
  • Agentic search with re-ranking
  • Low latency, high accuracy
  • Start free, scale to paid
  • No infrastructure management required

Our Mission

Making AI agents actually useful through better knowledge management. We're democratizing access to production-grade knowledge infrastructure, enabling rapid AI agent development without the complexity of building custom RAG systems.

Every AI agent should have access to shared, persistent knowledge. Every team building agents should be able to focus on their core product, not on building and maintaining knowledge infrastructure.

Why Usable Sp/f

Our three offerings work together to provide a complete solution:

  • Flowcore provides the underlying event storage and processing infrastructure
  • Usable provides the knowledge management layer built on Flowcore, optimized for AI agents
  • Consultancy helps organizations implement solutions, migrate data, and build custom AI and data systems

Together, we're building the infrastructure that makes AI agents truly useful—not just impressive demos, but production-ready tools that teams can rely on.
