Usable Chat Now Lives Inside Microsoft 365

July 6, 2026 Usable Team

Your AI shouldn't make you leave the document you're working in. Now it doesn't have to — Usable Chat works right inside Microsoft 365.

You're deep in a Word document, an Excel model, a PowerPoint deck, or an Outlook thread. You have a question for your AI — so you switch tabs, lose your place, copy-paste some context, and switch back. Every time.

That little bit of friction adds up. So we removed it.

Usable Chat, right where you work

Usable Chat now lives inside Microsoft 365. Ask a question, draft a paragraph, summarise a thread, or crunch some numbers — without ever leaving Word, Excel, PowerPoint, or Outlook.

And because it's Usable Chat, it comes with the thing that makes Usable different: long-term memory. It remembers your workspace, your projects, and what you've worked on before — so you're not re-explaining context every time. Everything you've built with Usable is available right where you're writing.

What you can do with it

  • In Word — draft, rewrite, and tighten copy using context from your workspace, not a blank page.
  • In Excel — ask questions about your data and get help building formulas and summaries in plain language.
  • In PowerPoint — turn rough notes into a first-draft deck, or reshape the slides you already have.
  • In Outlook — turn a long thread into a clear reply, or pull the action items out of an inbox mess.

All of it grounded in your own long-term memory — so the answers actually know your world.

On your terms

You stay in control the whole way: access is granted per app, there's zero data retention, and you can revoke it anytime. Install it where you want it, and remove it just as easily.

It works with the Microsoft 365 account and apps you already use — no new workflow to learn.
