Usable Fragments 2026

November 19, 2025 Tórshavn, Faroe Islands

Usable announces Usable Fragments - Tonik side-event, a gathering of the global AI agent builder community on 7 May 2026, 09:00-12:00 at Reinsaríið, Tórshavn.

TÓRSHAVN, Faroe Islands – November 19, 2025 – Usable today announced Usable Fragments - Tonik side-event, a gathering for the global AI agent builder community. The event will take place on 7 May 2026, 09:00-12:00 at Reinsaríið, Tórshavn, bringing together developers, founders, and knowledge workers who are defining the future of AI agents.

"Usable Fragments isn't just another tech event—it's an immersive experience for the global AI agent builder community," said Ólavur Ellefsen, CEO of Usable. "We're bringing developers, founders, and knowledge workers together in one of the most spectacular places on Earth to explore Usable technology and world-class knowledge management. This is where the future of AI agents gets built."

What to Expect

Usable Fragments - Tonik side-event will feature inspiration, deep-dive workshops, and meaningful networking against the dramatic backdrop of the Faroe Islands. Attendees will connect with peers, share breakthroughs, and discover how to build better AI agents in an environment that inspires fresh thinking and new perspectives.

The conference is designed for professionals building AI-powered solutions, including:

  • AI Agent Developers – Developers building AI agents and looking to improve their knowledge management capabilities
  • Founders & Product Leaders – Founders and product leaders integrating AI agents into their products
  • Knowledge Workers – Knowledge workers and technical writers managing organizational knowledge for AI systems

About Usable Sp/f

Usable (formerly Flowcore) is a Faroese technology company building developer-first knowledge management infrastructure for AI agents. The company provides the shared memory layer that enables agents to access structured, persistent knowledge across systems and sessions. Usable serves startups and developer teams globally from its base in the Faroe Islands. Website: https://www.usable.dev

Usable was founded by Ólavur Ellefsen, Julius á Rógvi Biskopstø, and Brian Bischoff. The company employs 8 full-time employees.
