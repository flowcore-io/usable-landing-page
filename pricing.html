Frequently Asked Questions

How does billing work?

For Usable, workspace owners pay for storage and knowledge creation tokens. Individual users pay for their own knowledge search tokens when they exceed free tier limits. Flowcore pricing is per subscription tier. Consultancy is billed per project.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and billing is prorated.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards and can arrange invoicing for enterprise customers. Contact us for details.

Need a custom solution?

Contact us for enterprise pricing, custom plans, or consultancy services. We're happy to work with you to find the right solution.
