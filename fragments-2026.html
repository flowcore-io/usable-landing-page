About the Event

A side-event to Tonik 2026 that brought the global AI agent builder community together in Tórshavn, Faroe Islands.

Usable Fragments was a practical Thursday morning session for people who want AI to work from their organization's real knowledge, not from generic prompts. Each short slot was a concrete walk-through of a workflow: what context is captured, how it is governed, and how it makes agents and applications more useful over time.

Side-event to Tonik 2026, hosted at Reinsaríið against the dramatic backdrop of the Faroe Islands. Five demo-heavy walkthroughs from people shipping production AI workflows on Usable — the shared memory layer for AI agents.

The full morning is available to watch above. The programme is below for reference.
