About Fragments Conference

A 2-day gathering of the global AI agent builder community, held in the breathtaking Faroe Islands.

The Fragments Conference isn't just another tech event—it's an immersive experience for developers, founders, and knowledge workers defining the future of AI agents. We are bringing the global community together in one of the most spectacular places on Earth to explore Usable technology and world-class knowledge management.

Join us for two days of inspiration, deep-dive workshops, and meaningful networking against the dramatic backdrop of the Faroe Islands. Connect with peers, share breakthroughs, and discover how to build better AI agents in an environment that inspires fresh thinking and new perspectives.

More details about speakers, schedule, and registration will be announced soon. Join the waitlist to be the first to know when registration opens.

Who Should Attend

This conference is designed for professionals building AI-powered solutions. Whether you're just getting started with AI agents or looking to take your knowledge management to the next level, Fragments 2026 is the place to connect, learn, and grow.
