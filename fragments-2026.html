About Usable Fragments 2026

A side-event to Tonik, bringing together the global AI agent builder community in Tórshavn, Faroe Islands.

Usable Fragments is a practical Thursday morning session for people who want AI to work from their organization's real knowledge, not from generic prompts. Each short slot is a concrete walk-through of a workflow: what context is captured, how it is governed, and how it makes agents and applications more useful over time.

Side-event to Tonik 2026, hosted at Reinsaríið against the dramatic backdrop of the Faroe Islands. Six demo-heavy walkthroughs from people building production AI workflows on Usable — the shared memory layer for AI agents.

Come for a demo-heavy morning on turning scattered expertise into an AI-ready memory layer. Free to attend.
