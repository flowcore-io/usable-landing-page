Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Alminni.

How does Alminni work?
Usable uses AI to capture, compress, and organize your team's knowledge. It learns from your interactions and builds a searchable memory that grows smarter over time.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and are SOC 2 compliant. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and we never share your information with third parties.
Can I integrate with my existing tools?
Yes! Usable integrates with popular development tools like GitHub, Slack, Notion, and more. We're constantly adding new integrations based on user feedback.
How long does setup take?
You can get started with Usable in under 5 minutes. Simply sign up, connect your tools, and start capturing knowledge. No complex configuration required.
What if I need to export my data?
You own your data. You can export all your knowledge fragments at any time in multiple formats including JSON, CSV, and Markdown. No vendor lock-in.
